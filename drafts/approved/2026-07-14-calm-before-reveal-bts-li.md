Tomorrow we go public with the numbers. Tonight I deleted three posts.

Not because they were wrong. Because they were trying too hard.

One had a dramatic opener that didn't earn it. One explained governance in a way that sounded like a feature list. One ended with "this changes everything," which it doesn't.

The urge to oversell a milestone is real. Especially when you've spent months building toward it. But the whole point of this project is that the work speaks for itself.

So tomorrow, three posts go out. One LinkedIn reveal, one X thread, one blog post. All numbers traced to source files. No hype. No drama. Just what we built and what the scores actually say.

If the numbers hold up under scrutiny, that's the story. If they don't, that's the story too.
