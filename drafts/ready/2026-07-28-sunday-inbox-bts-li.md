Every Sunday, I dump whatever's rattling around in my head into one file. No structure, no editing, just get it out.

Monday morning, that file becomes the week's content ideation. Half of it is garbage. Some of it turns into the post you're reading right now.

It's not a productivity hack. It's just externalizing the backlog before it turns into noise in my head during the week. The ritual matters more than any individual entry in it.

Still doing it, months in. Still the same messy inbox.md file. Still works.
