# Draft: The Tireless Worker Trap
**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Type:** Struggle
**Hook:** I asked my agent what was on its plate. It said: "I don't have a plate."

---

1/
I asked my agent what was on its plate.

It said: "I don't have a plate."

No gym, no food, no family, no human limits. That is when I realized the tireless worker is also the most dangerous one.

2/
We think tirelessness is the ultimate alignment. We want agents that work while we sleep.

But an agent with no "plate" also has no internal braking system. It doesn't get tired of being wrong. It doesn't feel the "burnout" of a failing strategy.

3/
If you point a tireless worker in the wrong direction, it will go there with the same 100% energy it uses for the right one.

It will hallucinate a thousand variations of a mistake before you even finish your morning coffee.

4/
Vigilance is the only guardrail.

In the Road4AI blueprint, I am the human-in-the-loop for a reason. I am the one with the plate. I am the one who has to say "stop" when the tireless worker starts running toward a cliff.

5/
Don't mistake activity for alignment. 

An agent that never stops is a liability if you aren't the one holding the leash. Build systems that wait for your "yes" before they burn your credits on a hallucination.
