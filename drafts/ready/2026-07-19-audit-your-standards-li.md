Realized I was re-explaining the same preferences to every model, every session, for weeks. No em dashes. Confidence tags on every claim. Hard stop before file edits. I knew these cold. I'd just never written them anywhere the agents could read.

The fix wasn't a better model. It was auditing my own standards once and encoding them into something persistent.

Once that happened, individual session quality mattered less. The system carried the standard forward whether I was paying attention or not.

Treat AI dev as a system, not a sequence of prompts. Process ends up mattering more than the model ever did.
