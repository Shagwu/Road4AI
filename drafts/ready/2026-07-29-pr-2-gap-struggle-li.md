Six tasks shipped. Thirteen commits, sixty-nine files changed. Multi-domain validation passed 12 out of 12 on the benchmark suite.

And it merged without going through Karen.

Not because the work was bad. The work was fine. It merged clean because the adversarial review gate wasn't wired into that PR's path, and nobody caught it until after the fact.

This is the uncomfortable part of building governance into your own workflow: the gate only works if it's actually in the way. A review process that's easy to route around isn't a review process, it's a suggestion.

Retroactive audit is underway now. Documenting exactly where the gap was and fixing the path so it can't happen the same way twice. Not fun to write publicly. Writing it anyway.
