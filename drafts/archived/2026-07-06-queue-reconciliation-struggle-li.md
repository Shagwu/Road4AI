I thought 32 posts were live. Turns out the system scheduled them but never confirmed they shipped.

Here's what happened. Blotato got the posts. The queue marked them as scheduled. But nobody checked whether they actually went out. For weeks.

The queue said "scheduled." The drafts said "archived." Everything looked correct from the inside. But the confirmation step, the part where you verify the post hit the platform and grab the URL, never happened.

This is the gap between "I built a pipeline" and "the pipeline actually works." Scheduling is easy. Confirming is where the system breaks.

I spent an hour today reconciling 32 entries. Most had fired. A few might not have. I still can't tell without checking each platform manually.

The lesson: a pipeline without a feedback loop is just a list of hopes.
