The benchmark passed. The skill improved. Now I'm staring at a system that works and wondering what I'm supposed to do next.

SkillOpt took voice-match from 0.64 to 0.87. Governance held. No drift. No rule violations. The learning loop works.

And I'm stuck.

Not because something broke. Because something didn't. When you spend months building toward a milestone, the silence after you hit it is deafening. You expected fireworks. You got a green checkmark and a question: now what?

Phase 4 starts next week. Harvester, drift monitoring, real-time signal routing. New systems to build, new failures to find.

But today I'm sitting with the uncomfortable truth that the hardest part of building isn't the struggle. It's the moment after the struggle ends and you have to find a new one.
