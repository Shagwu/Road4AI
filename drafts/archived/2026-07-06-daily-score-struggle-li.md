I check one number every morning. Some days it drifts yellow. I still can't prompt it back.

For the last two weeks, I've been running the same benchmark against voice-match every day. Same 10 cases. Same baseline. Same local model.

Most days it comes back green. A few days it flickered yellow. Once it hit blue.

The hardest part isn't running the check. It's knowing when NOT to intervene. When the score dips 5%, the urge to tweak the prompt, adjust the rules, or swap the model is loud.

But the whole point of drift monitoring is to watch, not to chase. If the system is working, the score stays stable. If it doesn't, the system tells you something is wrong. Your job is to listen, not to react.

Discipline over optimization. That's the part nobody talks about when they build with AI.
