## Review Notes

What works in the source post:
- The opening line is memorable and human.
- The core insight is sharp: tirelessness is not alignment.
- The ending lands with a clean operator principle.

What needed adapting:
- The X/LinkedIn draft is too text-led for Instagram and short video.
- The middle section explains the lesson, but the visual platforms need a clearer scene change.
- The CTA is minimal. Instagram and short-form video need a stronger invitation to respond.

agents do not get tired of being wrong.

Build the system.
Stay the pilot.

## Threads Version

I asked my agent what was on its plate today.

It said: "I don't have a plate."

That line stayed with me because it exposed a bigger problem:

agents do not get tired of being wrong.

Humans slow down.
Humans hesitate.
Humans notice friction.

Agents can sprint in the wrong direction with perfect consistency.

So no, speed is not the main win.
Vigilance is.

Build the system.
Stay the pilot.
