# LinkedIn Draft: The $0.60 Skill Evolution

We tested Microsoft's SkillOpt locally before letting Hermes depend on it.

I expected the validation to be expensive.

It cost about $0.30 to $0.60 for a small domain run.

More importantly, the optimizer produced coherent, reviewable skill edits without touching the governance boundary.

That is the v2.1 pattern:

Suggest.
Measure.
Review.
Then accept or reject.

No auto-merge.
No hidden self-modification.
No "the agent improved itself overnight" theater.

Skill evolution should be boring enough to trust.
