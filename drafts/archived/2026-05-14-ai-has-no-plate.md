## The Content

It said: "I don't have a plate."

No gym. No food. No family. No human limits. 

We talk about "agent alignment" like it is a math problem. But this week I realized tirelessness is the biggest risk of all. 

An agent does not get bored of going in the wrong direction. It does not get tired of being wrong. It just runs the loop.

If you point a swarm at a wall, it will hit that wall at 100mph and then ask for more battery. 

Vigilance is the only real guardrail. If you are not watching the loop, the loop is watching you.
