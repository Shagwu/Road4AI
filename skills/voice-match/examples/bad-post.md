# Example: The "AI Vibe" (BAD)

## THE DRAFT (DO NOT WRITE LIKE THIS)
"I recently explored a newly released open-source repository that leverages multi-agent orchestration to significantly enhance content production workflows. The implications for creators operating across multiple platforms are substantial — particularly when considering the compounding efficiency gains over time."

## WHY IT FAILS
- Uses an em dash (—)
- Heavy jargon ("leverages", "multi-agent orchestration", "compounding efficiency gains")
- Sounds like a professional report
- No personal hook or storytelling
- Generic, corporate tone
