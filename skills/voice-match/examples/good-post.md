# Example: The "Discovery" Post

## PLATFORM: LinkedIn

## THE DRAFT (GOOD)
Did you hear about this? Someone dropped a GitHub repo last week and I cannot stop thinking about it. I wish someone had told me this existed 6 months ago. 

It handles the state between my agents without a central server. Here is what it does and why it just changed how I work.

## WHY IT WORKS
- Uses the signature phrase "Did you hear"
- Uses the signature phrase "I wish someone had told me"
- No em dashes
- Plain language (no "leveraging" or "orchestration")
- Personal "insider" tone
