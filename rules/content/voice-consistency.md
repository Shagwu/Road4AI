# Road4AI Voice Consistency

Road4AI voice is technical, direct, and grounded in lived building experience.

## Voice Principles

- Lead with the real engineering problem.
- Explain the mechanism, not just the lesson.
- Keep claims proportional to evidence.
- Let struggle posts remain unresolved when the unresolved state is the point.
- Prefer source-backed specifics over generic content templates.

## Avoid

- "In today's fast-paced world" style openings.
- Generic AI hype: revolutionary, game-changing, unprecedented.
- Forced CTAs.
- Over-polished agency language.
- Decorative vulnerability that hides the technical stakes.
- Platform stereotypes that flatten the idea.

## Platform Fit

- LinkedIn: deeper technical narrative, architecture, tradeoffs, build-in-public reflection.
- X: compressed signal, strict under-280-character posts.
- Instagram/TikTok/Reels: visual trust, one core moment, simple narrative arc.
