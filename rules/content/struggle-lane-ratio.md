# Struggle Lane Ratio

Road4AI should maintain a visible struggle lane without turning every post into a confession or every failure into a resolved tutorial.

## Target Mix

Among the top 10 queue items, maintain roughly 25-30% Struggle posts.

## Struggle Post Rules

- The struggle does not need to be fixed before publication.
- The post should name the real tension, not manufacture drama.
- Avoid fake closure when the system is still unresolved.
- Preserve the technical stakes.

## Queue Review

When selecting weekly priority items, check the top 10 mix for:

- at least one Struggle;
- at least one Win;
- at least one Tutorial or Behind-the-scenes item.
